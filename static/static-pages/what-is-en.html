How it works.

  • 1
    Find a great project that makes you say "yes!"
    From a solar lights project in Mali to a bee-keeping project in Cambodia, there's a whole world of inspiring projects on 1%Club!
  • 2
    Support it with your money, skills or time.
    Each project needs something different to reach its target. You can support a project by either giving it money and/or offering your skills.
  • 3
    Follow the project's process.
    The project owners will happily keep you up to date on the project's progress by sharing blog posts, photos and videos.

• About the contest

    FMO & 1%Club present: the accelerate your smart idea contest! FMO proudly supports 1%Club in its mission to empower entrepreneurs around the world to develop themselves and the communities around them through social enterprises. Through the Arthur Arnold Foundation (AAF) we will award two entrepreneurs with grant money to finance their social enterprises. The winners will be selected within two months after the 1%Co-Creation Lab which will take place in October at 1%Club in Amsterdam.

  • 1%Co-Creation Lab

    Part of the contest was a co-creation to create solutions for challenges of the 6 participating projects of this FMO contest. On October 3rd, 60 professionals from FMO, Schiphol Group, Accenture and Frog Design shared expertise and insights with project owners who joined online. The result: read this blog and watch the video.

  • Rules of the game

    • The winner of the contest will win $7.000 dollars
    • The runner up will win $3.000
    • All finalists will join the 1%Co-Creation Lab on October 3rd to present their biggest challenge for companies, including FMO and Schiphol Group, to work on
    • All finalists will have until the end of November to crowdfund as much as possible
    • The winners will be chosen by FMO and AAF, and will base their decision on the following criteria: how scalable the project is, how innovative the project is, how entrepreneurial the smart idea is, and the results of the crowdfunding campaign

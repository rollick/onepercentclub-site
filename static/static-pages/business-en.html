How can your business
collaborate with 1%club

Mainly it comes down to supporting projects financially and/or sharing your professional expertise with young entrepreneurs from developing countries.

But, we wouldn't be 1%Club if we didn't come up with a bunch of smart, pragmatic and fun ways of doing this:



  • 1%Profit

    Share 1% of your profits, sales, bonus (and so on) in a structural way with projects that match your organisation’s goals. As a 1%PROFIT member we will share the impact of your 1% via our online communication channels. Get in touch!



  • 1%Giftcards

    Give 1%Giftcards to your employees or customers and let them choose themselves which project to support. It’s a great alternative for Christmas presents (for example) and an easy way to support projects that matter.


  • Cause
    Campaigning

    1%Club connects you with projects worldwide that match any theme you wish to focus on. We’ll provide your organization with inspiring projects, the right expertise and online tools to implement your campaign that matters! Get in touch & start your campaign!


  • 1%Co
    Creation

    During one day co-creation events up to 60 employees from 4 organisations work directly with 6 entrepreneurs from developing countries on their smart ideas. It’s an inspiring, involving, fulfilling day where a small effort has a big impact! Join a co-creation lab!


  • Employee Engagement Program

    1%Club offers a wide range of online and offline activities where employees share money,expertise, and/or time with our projects worldwide. Your employees will be engaged in socially responsible activities. Together we design the program that works best for us both! Let’s meet right away!

Why work together?

It's a proven concept

We are the world's biggest crowdsourcing platform to cope with global challenges - there are more than 14,000 1%Club members who have helped realize over 450 projects in 68 countries, directly improving thousands of lives.

We match your purpose

As 1%Club is active worldwide and covers a huge range of themes from water projects in Uganda to solar power projects in Nepal, you can select projects that fit your organisation's purpose and support them exactly how you wish.

We have a strong network

We believe passionately in the power of collaboration as a force for change. And we're glad that some of the world's biggest and most progressive companies think so too. Meet some of our partners here.

We are innovative together

We believe in a new way of international cooperation. We like to think of it as a global lab where we test new ideas, experiment with new technologies like live streaming while working with people all over the world.

Business meets society

We see Corporate Responsibility as a means to transform into a fully conscious, sustainable business. We offer all kinds of tools to guide you in this process and enrich your relationship with society and engage your stakeholders.
